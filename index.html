Educational Programs

Arabic language teaching program

We have an integrated curriculum for teaching Arabic to non-native speakers. We start with children from 4 years old, and we also start with all ages and all levels We also start with students who do not read, write, or understand the Arabic language from the introductory level, starting with the letters, and after they are able to know, read, and write them correctly and well, they move to the next level..

Read More

The Holy Quran program

If the students are not good at reading, we start with them from the book Nour al-Bayan, Fath al-Rahman, or al-Qaeda al-Baghdadi. The goal of studying these books is for the child to read in a correct and sound manner from the Holy Qur’an We also begin with the child to memorize the Qur’an if he is unable to read by rote learning and understand the meanings of words and interpret them. This helps to memorize the Qur’an and establish it in the child’s mind..

Sharia sciences education program
